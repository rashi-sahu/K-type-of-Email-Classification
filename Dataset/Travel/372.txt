klassow ha hwarvosow, y'ga mysk an Benn-seythen Gernewek, an brassa kuntelles  bledhynnyek a gernewegoryon.

Kowethas
an Yeth Kernewek is a registered charity which promotes the Cornish language
through a wide range of publications, classes and events including the Penn-seythen Gernewek, the largest annual gathering of Cornish speakers.
Cornwall
and its language have been inextricably linked throughout history and its
presence can still be seen all around in place names, family names and in
dialect.
Henry
Jenner, pivotal Cornish revivalist, said, "Why should Cornishmen learn
Cornish? ... Because they are Cornishmen."
