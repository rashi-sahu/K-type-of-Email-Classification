Welcome to Trethevy Farm A traditional Cornish Farmhouse Bed and Breakfast on Bodmin Moor.A warm and friendly welcome awaits you at Trethevy Farm:Listed 16th Century farmhouse in peaceful countrysideWi-fi, tea & coffee making facilities, colour TV in each roomEnjoy comfortable rooms, a hearty farmhouse breakfast and a proper Cornish welcome Thank you for making us so welcome and for your excellent breakfasts. What a superb country setting quiet, friendly and comfortable.
Contact us on: 01579 343186
Why Trethevy Farm?Perfect for walkers – footpaths on the doorstep, and we are just a stone’s throw from Trethevy QuoitExplore Bodmin Moor and Poldark country: rambling, horseriding, cycling, birdwatching, exploring ancient monuments, watersports close by at Siblyback LakeWithin easy reach of the Cornish coast: the fishing villages of Looe and Polperro are a short drive An ideal location for visiting Cornwall’s attractions, including the Eden Project, Lost Gardens of Heligan, National Trust properties at Cotehele and LanhydrockOr just relax, enjoy the peace and quiet, the wildlife and the glorious countryside.Trethevy Farm – Traditional Cornish Farmhouse Bed and Breakfast on Bodmin Moor
