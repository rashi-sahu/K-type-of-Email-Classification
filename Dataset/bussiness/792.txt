Who we are
We unlock the power of data to create opportunities for people, business and society. At life’s big moments – from buying a home or a car, to sending a child to college, to growing your business by connecting it with new customers – we help you to manage your data with confidence so you can make the most of every opportunity.
Read more about us here
Our differentiators and fundamentals
Identity and access to credit
We help people improve their financial identities and access to credit.
Financial education
We work in partnership to provide credit and financial education to people, communities and schools.
Debt
We help tackle unmanageable debt among vulnerable groups.
Data
We protect and manage our data securely and responsibly. Data quality and accuracy is fundamental to our business.
Environment
We operate efficiently and minimise our impact on the environment.
Employees
We strive to create a great place to work for our people.

About Experian
Experian is the world’s leading global information services company. At life’s big moments – from buying a home or car, to sending a child to college, to growing a business by connecting it with new customers – we empower consumers and our clients to manage their data with confidence.
Working at Experian
Working at Experian, you’ll be amongst smart, passionate people who will collaborate with you to help you be your best too. To find out more, look at our unique and consistent way of working globally; we call it ‘The ‘Experian Way’. It informs how our people act and behave, which shapes our culture.
Business areas
There is a world of opportunity to be found in many different roles at Experian. People come to work with us to stretch their abilities and push the boundaries. The roles and possibilities are almost limitless – explore our business areas and see what’s right for you.
Global Opportunities
As a global brand, we’re all about changing things for the better. Leading change takes a particular type of person. Are you ready to take on the challenge? Let’s create a better tomorrow together.
