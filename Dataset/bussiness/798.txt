Only the adaptive survive

Humans are smart. We’re naturally programmed to do Adaptive Behavioural Analytics. We intuitively profile the people we know over time – continually adapting our sense of a normal pattern of behaviour for each individual. So if we spot someone behaving out of character, we reach out to help.

Real life breaks the rules
Single Edit
Column Edit
Featurespace’s smart technology works in exactly the same way. Deployed on-premise or via secure cloud in over 180 countries, our ARIC (adaptive, real-time, individual, change) platform uses Bayesian statistics to model and predict individual behaviour in real-time. This machine learning allows computers – for the first time – to understand when an individual customer’s behaviour is out of character and automatically evaluate risk.See how it works

Featurespace monitors all your customer data in real-time, spotting anomalies to block new fraud attacks as they occur. At the same time, Featurespace recognises your genuine customers without blocking their activity. It all means reduced fraud costs, happier customers, and increased revenue.
