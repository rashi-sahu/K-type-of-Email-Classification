DENMAR inflatable stoppers manufactured in the UK
Inflatable Pipeline Stoppers manufactured in sizes 2" - 84" in canvas or nylon, complete with hose and brass
tap as standard.  Schrader valves and larger sizes are also available
Specification for Denmar Inflatable Stoppers
Bladders - All are manufactured from either dipped rubber latex or latex sheet.
Tubing - PVC braided tubing is used throughout the range.  Tube length is 6ft long on sizes 2" - 18",
and 4ft 6in on larger sizes.
Valves - 1/4" BSP female brass flexible tube cock, or schrader valve (car tyre type) please state when ordering.
1/2" ball valve can be supplied on larger bags.
Cases - Made in either PU coated polyester cotton, weight 14oz per square metre or PU coated nylon in either
4oz per sq mtr or 6oz for heavy duty.  (Plus 24 grammes per sq mtr PU coating)
Inflation Pressures - 2 - 4" = 10 psi (pounds per sq inch).     6" = 6 psi.
8 - 10" = 4 psi.  12 - 16" = 3 psi.  18" and over = 2 psi
