HET Documents
Documents for the History of Economics
This is a (more or less up to date) copy of the
collection of documents
created by Rod Hay at McMaster University
in Canada. It also includes French language documents created by Paulette Taieb
and others, and bibliographies constructed by George Zizka. This site is
maintained by Tony Brewer
in the Economics Department
of the University of Bristol. Other possibilities are Robert Dixon's site at
Melbourne and Michio
Akama's site in Japan, which includes brief biographical and reference
material on authors featured. For French language texts, and much else, see Paulette Taieb's site or the site of the Centre de l'Histoire de
la Pensée Economique in Paris.
For a wider range of links relevant to the history of economics see here or here. Other document
collections here.
