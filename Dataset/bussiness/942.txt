JDC Management Consulting is an international
network of senior and experienced specialists who provide independent
management consultancy in Life Sciences R&D.
The overall theme of JDC Management Consulting services centres on assistance
to R&D which will help clients in the 'Application of Judgement for
good Decisions with the necessary Commitment'.
Services scope includes the development of clear strategy and coherent
follow through into effective execution.
Key benefits from working with JDC include:
Specific experience of working with Life Sciences
R&D for over 12 years
Independent, informed and impartial advice
Practical and creative redesign, re-engineering and
change management
Coaching from our experience to help others avoid
pitfalls
Access to wide specialised expertise as required
through the JDC network
