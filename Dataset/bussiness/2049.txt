Welcome to the Quakernomics Blog
The Quakernomics blog is a forum for discussion and debate about the relationships between economics, our lives, and the planet. It is a space to both reflect on and plan ways to build a more just and sustainable world.It has been set up by Quaker Peace & Social Witness (QPSW) as a space for Friends and others to share ideas, debate, learn and be inspired to take action.
Please read the blogs and add your comments. If you would like to start an additional thread (subject for discussion) then all you need to do is register as a blogger and you can add your post. It will only take a couple of minutes to register.
