j4p is a recruitment
consultancy run by skilled and experienced product professionals.
We specialise in matching the skills of our recruits to those of our
clients who are typically involved in all aspects of product and
business creation from research through development and manufacture
to sales and support.
We focus on companies developing products and services for
telecommunications markets with particular emphasis on the fixed and
mobile wireless sectors.
Our backgrounds in all the elements of the product life cycle enable
us to handle all the disciplines and the skills that our clients are
seeking.
