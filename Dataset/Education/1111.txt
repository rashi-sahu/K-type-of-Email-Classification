Data protection
The Data Protection Act 1998 gives rights to people about whom we hold information, and gives us responsibilities regarding that information.
Freedom of information
The Freedom of Information (Scotland) Act 2002 gives anyone the right to request access to recorded information held by the University.
Records management
Good records management helps you to do your job, saves time and improves accountability. We can help you to implement records management in your business area.
