Zantiks Ltd
Cambridge Studies
Info
Cambridge Studies Limited is a company based in the UK, providing both national and international consultancy services in the areas of education, entrepreneurship, innovation, knowledge transfer, research and development (R&D), renewable energies, biotechnology and ICT.
Our main work focuses on supporting the internationalization of small and medium enterprises (SMEs) from both the Canary Islands region (in particular) and other Spanish regions, and the search for partners and investors for international innovation projects. In this regard, Cambridge Studies collaborates with a significant number of entities, both private and public (knowledge-intensive production spaces, clusters, technology parks, Universities, research organizations, etc.).
Cambridge Studies Ltd. also deals with enquires and applications from internationals students that would like to study in Cambridge. We offer a wide range of taught courses such as English studies, foundation programmes to postgraduate courses and research opportunities (MA, MBA, PhDs) across the arts, humanities, science and social science subject areas in an interdisciplinary study as well as single-subject study.
