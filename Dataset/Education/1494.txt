eMargin is an online collaborative annotation tool. You can highlight, colour-code, write notes and assign tags to individual words or passages of a text. These annotations can be shared amongst groups, generating discussions and allowing analyses and interpretations to be combined.
It's free to use and you can give it a try right now...
eMargin is being developed by the Research and Development Unit for English Studies at Birmingham City University.
