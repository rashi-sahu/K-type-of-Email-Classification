Corporate Social Responsibility Report 2016
Walgreens Boots Alliance is committed to be a global leader in supporting people and the planet, with a focus on serving our communities, environment, marketplace and workplace
Walgreens Boots Alliance
Walgreens Boots Alliance is the first global pharmacy-led, health and wellbeing enterprise. Our purpose is to help people across the world lead healthier and happier lives.
Retail Pharmacy USA
Our Retail Pharmacy USA Division’s principal retail pharmacy brands are Walgreens and Duane Reade. Together, they form one of the largest drugstore chains in the USA.
Retail Pharmacy International
Our Retail Pharmacy International Division has pharmacy-led health and beauty retail businesses in eight countries. In Europe, we are a market leader.
Pharmaceutical Wholesale
Our Pharmaceutical Wholesale Division supplies medicines and other healthcare products and related services to pharmacies, doctors, health centers and hospitals.
