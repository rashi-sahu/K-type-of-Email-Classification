Clinical Sciences Library has moved
The Clinical Sciences Library has moved to the David Wilson Library
on the main University of Leicester campus - where University staff and
students can continue to access the same range of information resources
and services.
At the David Wilson Library you will find:
The medical books on the Floor 1, yellow zoneThe medical reference collection is in the Express Zone, Ground FloorThe print medical journals are in the BasementYour reservations and inter-library loans are available for collection from the Express Zone and Service Desk.
The Clinical Sciences Library space in the Robert Kilpatrick Clinical
Sciences Building will reopen as study space for University of Leicester
medical students from Monday 22 August 2011. 24/7 access, University
computing and printing facilities will remain.
NHS staff with honorary University of Leicester staff status
You have full access to the University Library, including digital resources.  For more information, please visit Joining the Library.
NHS staff
NHS users are advised to contact the University Hospitals of Leicester NHS Trust (UHL) Library Service
about their postal loan service.  For details please contact: Glenfield
Hospital Library, Tel: 0116 256 3672, Email: ggh.library@uhl-tr.nhs.uk
or Leicester General Hospital Library, Tel: 0116 258 4245, Email:
leicslib@uhl-tr.nhs.uk.
