Welcome to the website for Critical Approaches to Discourse Analysis across Disciplines (CADAAD).  CADAAD is an open community of researchers who are critically engaged with discourse.  CADAAD is inter-disciplinary and aims to promote a broad range of topics and approaches in critical discourse research.
CADAAD organises biannual conferences which run in every even year.  The most recent conference was held in Catania, Sicily in 2016.
