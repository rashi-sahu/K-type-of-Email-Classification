Autumn Statement 2012 (Archived)
The Chancellor of the Exchequer delivered his Autumn Statement to Parliament on 5 December 2012.
The government’s economic strategy is focused on reducing the deficit, restoring stability, rebalancing the economy and equipping the UK to compete in the global race. The Autumn Statement sets out further action in 3 areas: protecting the economy, growth, and fairness.
Autumn Statement 2013 will take place on 5 December 2013.
