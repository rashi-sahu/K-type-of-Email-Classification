Welcome to the London Irish Business Society.
LIBS was established in 2009 and is the premiere organisation for Irish professionals living in London.
LIBS provides a forum for Irish professionals to network and share ideas. We run several events for our members throughout the year. We issue regular communications to our members about upcoming events so to ensure you do not miss out please join LIBS, it's free! just click here to join.
Alternatively, you can join us on Twitter or Linked In.
If you interested in getting in contact with the London Irish Business Society please fill out the below form or contact us via e-mail on info@li-bs.co.uk.
