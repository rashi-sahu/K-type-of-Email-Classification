Budget 2012: How will the budget affect you? 21 March 2012 Last updated at 22:27 GMT
George Osborne has cut the 50p top rate and the amount of income tax millions of lower earners pay, but pensioners face a future hit from the Budget.The BBC's Danny Savage spent the day in Northallerton in North Yorkshire, where he canvassed opinion on the changes laid out by the Chancellor.The BBC's Budget calculator
