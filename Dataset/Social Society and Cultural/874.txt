Awards
WV11 .co.uk is a volunteer run award winning website dedicated to community news and events in and around Wednesfield, Wolverhampton.
In 2011 we were given a 'Pride of Wolverhampton' award by local radio station Signal 107 and in 2012 we were given the 'Community Engagement' award by Talk About Local.
