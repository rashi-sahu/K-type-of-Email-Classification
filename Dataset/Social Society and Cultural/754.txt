PUDDING MILL RIVER
Pudding Mill River: Purveyors of Sporting Spirits and Foodstuffs have been gathering the wild fruits of the Lower Lea Valley for generations. Now that the Games are coming to town they are hard at work collecting the last wild harvest (from blackberries to sloes) from the very edges of the Olympic zone.
